# Banking Loan Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
A comprehensive financial data analysis project focused on evaluating banking loan portfolios, assessing risk metrics such as Debt-to-Income (DTI) and interest rates, and analyzing applicant demographics. Designed with a professional blue-themed UI, this dashboard provides actionable insights into loan performance, regional trends, and borrower behaviors to optimize lending strategies.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

1- **Data Analysis & Visualization:** Microsoft Excel (Advanced Formulas, Pivot Tables, Dynamic Charts).

2- **Data Cleaning & Structuring:** Power Query.


## 📊 Core KPIs

1- **Total Loan Amount:** $435.8 M.

2- **Total Received Amount:** $473.1 M.

3- **Total Loan Applications:** 38.6K.

4- **Average Interest Rate:** 12.05%.

5- **Average DTI (Debt-to-Income):** 13.33%.


## 💡 Key Business Insights

1- **Loan Status & Risk:** Out of 38.6K total applications, 32.1K are "Fully Paid," yielding $411.6 M in received amounts against a $351.4 M loaned amount. Conversely, 5.3K loans are "Charged Off," resulting in a loss where only $37.3 M was received against a loaned amount of $65.5 M.

2- **Interest & DTI Impact:** "Charged Off" (defaulted) loans are consistently associated with higher average interest rates (13.88%) and higher average DTI (14.00%) compared to "Fully Paid" loans, which average 11.64% interest and 13.17% DTI. Current active loans exhibit the highest metrics, standing at 15.10% interest and 14.72% DTI.

3- **Borrower Demographics:** The vast majority of loan amounts are issued to borrowers holding a Mortgage ($219.3 M) or those who Rent ($185.8 M), while outright homeowners account for only $29.6 M. Additionally, applicants with 10+ years of employment length are the most frequent borrowers, submitting 8.9K applications.

4- **Loan Characteristics:** 73% of all loan applications are structured for a 36-month term, whereas 27% are for a 60-month term. The most prominent purpose for borrowing is Debt Consolidation, followed distantly by Credit Card (5.0K applications) and Home Improvement (2.9K applications).

5- **Geographic & Temporal Trends:** California (CA) is the leading state for applications with 6.9K, followed by New York (NY) at 3.7K and Florida (FL) at 2.8K. Furthermore, monthly loan applications demonstrate a consistent upward trajectory throughout the year, growing steadily from 2.3K in January to a peak of 4.3K in December.


## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

1- **Risk Mitigation:** Implement stricter approval thresholds and manual reviews for applicants approaching a 14% DTI, as this metric strongly aligns with the average profile of "Charged Off" accounts.
2- **Product Focus:** Since 73% of the loan volume is driven by 36-month terms and the primary use case is Debt Consolidation, the bank should consider offering specialized, highly competitive 3-year debt consolidation packages to capture even more market share safely.
3- **Targeted Marketing:** Focus marketing campaigns and promotional loan offers on professionals with 10+ years of employment in high-volume states like California (CA) and New York (NY) to maximize acquisition efficiency.
